PPE Risk Calculator: Respirator Performance

The PPE Risk Calculator is a tool using a computationally-based model to conservatively estimate PPE performance. Overall filtration efficiency as well as log10 reduction per particle size range is calculated using material characterstics of polypropylene-based respirators and masks.

Modules

• Respirator Performance Risk Calculator

A tool to conduct screening level risk assessment of surgical respirators and surgical masks to aid in the performance evaluation of textile materials used in the construction of surgical respirators and surgical masks. These assessments can assist PPE manufacturers by providing instantaneous feedback on whether their textile materials would require additional justification and/or testing to demonstrate acceptable performance.
